About Us

BloodLoof facilitates blood donation, blood donors register to donate blood, and hospitals register to collect blood from donors and hospitals. The main objectives of BloodLoof are to reduce the blood shortage in Rwanda, facilitate blood donations, and educate Rwandans about non-emergency blood collection.
